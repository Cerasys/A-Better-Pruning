About Us

Since 1981, A Better Pruning has proudly served the greater north Seattle area with exceptional landscaping and small tree services. Transitioning into a new era of leadership, Joe Berry, son of the founder Mike Berry, has seamlessly taken over the reins of the business. Building on a legacy of quality and expertise, Joe is committed to upholding the high standards set by his father.

With over 35 years of family tradition, A Better Pruning continues to be dedicated to enhancing the beauty of yards in the Pacific Northwest. Joe Berry brings a fresh perspective and a deep understanding of the local climate and environment. Born and raised in Seattle, he appreciates the unique challenges and opportunities that come with landscaping in this region.

A Better Pruning has evolved from its humble beginnings, starting with Mike and a push lawn mower in the back of his station wagon. Today, under Joe's leadership, it has grown into a full-service landscape and tree service company with multiple trucks and state-of-the-art equipment capable of handling any job.

Maintaining the customer-centric approach established by his father, Joe is an outgoing professional who values each individual customer. Before every project, he conducts a free walk-around estimate to personally connect with clients and understand the specific needs of their yards.

A Better Pruning, now under the guidance of Joe Berry, remains dedicated to delivering superior service, transforming overgrown hedges, trees, and bushes into stunning components that enhance the beauty of every yard.
