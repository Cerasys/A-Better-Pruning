About Us

Since 1981, Michael Berry has been serving the greater north Seattle area with superior landscaping and small tree services. Mike is all about quality. With over 35 years of experience, he has developed an eye for how to shape a beautiful yard. Somewhere within your overgrown hedge, tree, or bush is a stunning component to your yard, and Michael knows how to find it. He has gone from a push lawn mower in the back of his station wagon to a full service landscape and tree service company with multiple trucks and the equipment to do any job. He is a born-and-raised Seattle native that understands the climate and environment of the Pacific Northwest. He is an extrovert that truly cares for each individual customer. Before every job, Michael does a free walk-around estimate so he gets a chance to know you and the specific needs of your yard.
